Ding Wangbin graduated from Fuzhou University with a major in Artificial Intelligence. Currently, he is employed at Fujian Medical University and serves as a co-founder at Fuzhou Insense Technology Co., Ltd. In recent years, his primary research focus has been on multimodal cardiac image registration, multimodal cardiac image segmentation, and analysis of acute myocardial infarction lesions in multi-sequence MRI. In the interdisciplinary field of medical imaging and artificial intelligence, Ding Wangbin has contributed to over 10 publications in top international academic conferences and journals such as MICCAI, IEEE JBHI, IEEE TMI, and MedIA.
